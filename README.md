# 📄 AI Resume Analyzer

AI-powered resume parser that extracts skills, experience, education and scores resumes automatically.

**Built by Maheen — AI & ML Engineer**

---

## ✨ Features

- 📧 Extract emails & phone numbers
- 💻 Detect technical skills (30+ languages/tools)
- 🤝 Detect soft skills
- 🎓 Identify education & degrees
- 📅 Extract years of experience
- ⭐ Auto-score resume out of 100
- 📊 Generate JSON report
